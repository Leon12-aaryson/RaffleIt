How RafflesIt works

What is a 50/50 Raffle?

A 50/50 raffle is a fundraiser where upon completion of the raffle, half of the net proceeds are given to the charity that hosted the fundraiser and half is given to the randomly* selected winner.

*Winners are selected using a 3rd party, verified, and secure Random Number Generator.
