About RaffleIt

Do Good. Have Fun.

RafflesNow exists to help organizations easily
raise funds & for everyone to have fun along the way.

Born from the belief that we can all Do Good & Have Fun, RafflesNow has set out to revolutionize fundraising by providing a 50/50 raffle platform for the 21st century; less work for charities, more fun for donors.

By leveraging technology and social sharing, we're creating a new category of incentivized, gamified charitable giving that allows charities to easily host events that donors actively engage with, in hopes of potentially winning themselves.

Our online platform seamlessly connects organizations, clubs, charities and more with virtually a limitless audience made available through the power of mobile connectivity. This combined with an intuitive interface creates an engaging raffling experience both on and off-site, providing both charities and donors an exciting way to make a difference.

Additionally, while our platform allows for fundraising across state lines (where accepted) and in turn enhances the raffling experience and audience reach for charities, we are continually looking to further expand states' acceptance of this engaging and successful means of fundraising. As the world embraces technological advancements, we're curious why charities are held to such antiquated rules which prevent them from leveraging technology and ultimately raising more funds to make the world a better place.

It's our hope that as the world finds its new footing, charitable giving will be in stride with these advancements and it's why we've built our platform not only for today, but also for tomorrow. By providing an efficient and scalable solution for charities to easily host raffles and access new donors, we're excited to take part in re-writing how people fundraise while having fun in the process.
